About the Institute
Sri Adichunchanagiri Mahasamsthana Math is a spiritual and social centre with a history of over 1800 years. It has 500+ educational institutions that educate more than 200,000 students. Most institutions are located in rural parts of the country and offer education from kindergarten to PhD.

BGS College of Engineering and Technology (BGSCET) is a unit of Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust, founded in 2022. It is located in west Bangalore, home of iconic landmarks such as the Indian Institute of Science, the World Trade Centre, and the ISKCON Temple. The institution is easily accessible by Namma Metro and Majestic Bus Station, City, and Yeshwantpur railway junctions.
About the program
